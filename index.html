What is the I-10 Expansion

TThe I-10 TxDOT project is a highway expansion in Houston, planning to elevate I-10 and expand its lanes from Voss to I-45. This initiative aims to manage traffic but will significantly harm the city's landscape and infrastructure, while still not reducing congestion.

How will this affect Houston

The expansion will pollute bayous, divide communities, and lead to the destruction of homes and businesses. It threatens to disrupt the environmental and community cohesion in Houston, impacting the city negatively.

Our Proposal

We propose using the $347 million to cap I-10, connecting The Heights to Montrose, instead of expanding it. This plan includes enhancing public transit and flood protection measures, focusing on community connectivity and environmental safety.
